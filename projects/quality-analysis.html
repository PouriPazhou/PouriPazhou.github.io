Service Quality Analysis — Complaint Trends & Root Causes

Tools: Check sheet • Pareto • Run charts • Fishbone diagram

I reviewed seven weeks of customer service complaint data and used standard quality tools to identify the highest-impact issues and recommend improvements.

What I did

  • Created a check sheet to categorize and count complaint types
  • Built a Pareto analysis to identify the “vital few” drivers of dissatisfaction
  • Used run charts to visualize complaint trends over time
  • Created a fishbone diagram to map potential root causes

Key findings

  • 74 total complaints across seven weeks
  • Top issues: communication/behavior, furniture damage, scheduling
  • Top 3 categories accounted for ~78% of complaints

Recommendations

  • Regular training (handling items, professional communication)
  • Quality checks before/after service
  • Equipment maintenance and clear communication standards
  • Pricing transparency to reduce billing complaints
